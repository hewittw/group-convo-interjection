# Social Norms for Conversation
# These norms will be used by Gemini when generating intervention responses

## Core Norms:

1. **Respect yourself and others**
   - No self-deprecating language ("I'm stupid", "I can't do anything")
   - No insults or personal attacks
   - Treat everyone with kindness

2. **Positive collaboration**
   - Don't tell someone to stop speaking or be quiet

3. **Constructive communication**
   - Give helpful feedback
   - Disagree respectfully
   - Stay on topic

4. **Inclusive behavior**
   - Welcome different perspectives
   - No exclusionary language
   - Encourage participation from everyone

## Example Violations:
- "I'm so dumb"
- "You're stupid"
- "Nobody cares what you think"
- "That's a terrible idea"
- "Shut up"
